🦄 HOW TO PLAY

You are a 🦄 Unicorn trying to turn the whole Grid into 🍪 Cookies!

□ Tap a yellow number next to your Unicorn to bake that many Cookies in a row.

Your 🦄 moves to the end of the row. Keep chaining!

🛑 GAME OVER if your path hits a 👾 Monster, your own 🍪 trail, or the edge!

❓ HOW TO PLAY

🦄 Bake as many cookies as possible!

□ Tap a yellow cell next to your Unicorn

🔢 The number = cookies baked in that direction

🦄 Unicorn moves to end of row — keep chaining!

💥 GAME OVER IF YOU...

👾 Hit a Cookie Monster  🍪 Run into your trail

🚫 Fall off the edge  🛑 Run out of moves
