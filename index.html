📖 Details

🦄 Bake as many cookies as possible!

👾 Avoid the hidden Cookie Monsters!

□ Tap a yellow cell next to your unicorn

🔢 The number = cookies baked in that direction

🦄 Your unicorn moves to the end of the row

🔁 Keep chaining to bake as many as you can!

👾 Hit a Cookie Monster = GAME OVER

🍪 Run into your own Cookie trail = GAME OVER

🚫 Fall off the edge = GAME OVER

🛑 Run out of valid moves = GAME OVER

📅 Daily = same grid for everyone, new one each day!

🏆 Score = Cookies Baked ÷ Total Grid

🏆 HIGH SCORES!

✨ ABOUT ✨

Unicorns represent rare moments of feeling seen.

This game is a thank you to everyone who has ever shown up and made me feel seen.
